I was born into a nominally Catholic family, and loved being Catholic. When I was twelve I was argued out of Catholicism and into Protestantism. When I was thirteen I argued myself out of Christianity and into atheism. At sixteen I was argued into Mormonism. At twenty I was argued out of Mormonism and back into Christianity again. At twenty-four I was argued back into Catholicism. And I am reasonably convinced that I will stay Catholic until I die.

I've always figured that it's safer to follow my head than my heart. Logic shows us truth, and the truth won't change. Feelings always will.
